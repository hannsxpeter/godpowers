# MRR Tracker PRD

## Problem Statement

[DECISION] MRR Tracker helps solo SaaS founders review weekly Stripe MRR movement by separating new, expansion, contraction, and churn dollars into inspectable account lists.

## Users

[DECISION] The primary user is a solo SaaS founder who runs billing through Stripe and reviews revenue movement every Monday morning.

## Functional Requirements

- [DECISION] P-MUST-01 The dashboard must show total MRR change split into new, expansion, contraction, and churn dollars for the selected week.
- [DECISION] P-MUST-02 The dashboard must list the customer accounts behind each movement bucket with the exact dollar delta.
- [DECISION] P-SHOULD-01 The dashboard should export a weekly summary sentence that names the largest positive and negative account changes.

## Non-Functional Requirements

| Category | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Accuracy | [DECISION] Stripe subscription deltas must reconcile to the displayed total within 1 dollar for the selected week. |
| Privacy | [DECISION] Customer account names must stay local to the connected Stripe workspace. |

## Scope and No-Gos

### In scope for V1

- [DECISION] Weekly Stripe MRR movement for one workspace.
- [DECISION] Account-level drill-down for movement buckets.

### Explicitly NOT in scope

- [DECISION] Multi-currency consolidation is not in scope for V1.
- [DECISION] Enterprise forecasting is not in scope for V1.

## Success Metrics

- [DECISION] Within 30 days of V1 launch, 10 founders complete one weekly review using the dashboard, measured via local review-completed events.
- [DECISION] Within 60 days of V1 launch, 80 percent of completed reviews open at least one account drill-down, measured via local interaction events.
